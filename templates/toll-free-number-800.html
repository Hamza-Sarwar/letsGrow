What is the toll free number 800 service from Bevatel?

Bevatel provides this service through an easy-to-remember unified number that starts with 800 that enables you to connect all branches and offices of your establishment or business through one number to make and receive all your customers' calls from anywhere in Saudi Arabia.

  • Toll free number 800 enables your target customers to contact you for free, as the costs of any call received or made through this number are paid by your call center. This service facilitates communication for many customers and encourages customers to communicate with you.

  • This number also gives you many advantages and solutions that enable you to communicate professionally with your customers. Also, it helps you develop the performance of the call center and technical support for your establishment or business.

What is the difference between
the unified number 9200 service and the toll free number 800?

The number 800 provides you with the same benefits as the unified number 9200 to make and receive calls and to operate customer service and technical support in your organization or business. However, there is a difference in the mechanism for paying for calls. We explain it as follows:-

    Unified number

    9200

In the number 9200, the caller pay for half of the costs of the call. And your establishment pays for another half

    Toll Free Number

    800

The toll free number 800 enables your customers to call you for free. And your establishment deducts the costs of the calls entirely.

Live Panel
